A Brief Tutorial

Let's get oriented with some key terms

What we see above is a network. Networks are made of two types of elements: nodes and edges.

Nodes are the circle elements in the visualization. Nodes commonly denote anything that can be a “noun".

Edges are the line elements in the visualization, and they connect nodes. Edges commonly denote anything that can be a “verb”.
